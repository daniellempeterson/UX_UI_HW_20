Eco Geek App

Currently, there is a lack of knowledge and accessibility to environmentally friendly practices, such as recycling. We found that users want to become more eco-friendly, but are unsure of how to properly dispose of products and found it difficult to access local resources. The goal for the EcoGeek app is to help users easily and accurately discard products and access their local resources, thereby, improving personal satisfaction and helping save the planet.

It uses utility classes for typography and spacing to space content out within the larger container.
